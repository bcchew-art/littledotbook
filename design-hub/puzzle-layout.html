Merlion Sticker Puzzle

32-piece mosaic puzzle — match stickers to build the Merlion

A4 Pull-Out Design — Ready for Review

Section 01

How It Works

Three simple steps. Two play modes. One Merlion to build.

1
Peel
Peel a sticker from the sheet
2
Match
Find the matching zone by shadow or number
3
Stick
Place the sticker to fill the zone
Easy Mode
Ages 3–4: Match Numbers
Each sticker and puzzle zone has the same number printed on it. Find the matching number and stick.
Challenge Mode
Ages 5–6: Match Shadows
Each zone shows a faint ghost image of the sticker that belongs there. Use the shape and outline to figure out where to stick.
