Section 01

The Concept

Main Book

Singapore MRT Sticker Map

  • Full MRT network spread
  • Kids place landmark stickers at correct stations
  • Teaches geography & the train network
  • Interactive — learn while playing
Pull-Out

A4 Merlion Puzzle

  • 32 interlocking puzzle pieces
  • Each piece features a Singapore icon
  • Together they form the Merlion
  • Teaches iconic local symbols

Part of a Series

Volume 2 in the "My Singapore Stories" series, following Book 1: Kampung to City (Sticker Book), sold at Epigram Bookshop.

Fresh Visual Style

Totally new illustration direction — not following Book 1's look. A clean, joyful style designed specifically for this volume.

Made for Little Ones

Target age 4–8 years. Every element — icons, layout, puzzle pieces — sized and designed for small hands and curious minds.

Celebrate Singapore

From hawker food to HDB blocks, MRT trains to the Merlion — a love letter to everything that makes Singapore unique.
