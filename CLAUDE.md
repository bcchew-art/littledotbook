# Little Dot Book — Book 2 Design

## Project Overview
- **Authors:** Jackie Ang, Michele Ang
- **Publisher:** Little Dot Book
- **Series:** My Singapore Stories
- **Existing book:** "My Singapore Story: Kampung to City" — S$12.80 via Epigram Bookshop
- **Sales model:** B2B — wholesale to bookstores (Epigram, possibly others)

## Phase 1 (NOW): Book 2 Design — Print-Ready Artwork
Jackie's 2nd book in the "My Singapore Stories" series. Two components:

### A. Main Book — Singapore MRT Map
- Singapore MRT map as the backdrop
- Kids stick individual landmark stickers along the MRT lines
- Landmarks: Esplanade, MBS, kopitiam, HDB, etc.
- Educational: learn Singapore geography through MRT network

### B. Pull-Out — Merlion Puzzle (A4)
- A4-size pull-out page
- Merlion silhouette made up of 32 interlocking puzzle pieces
- Each puzzle piece is a different Singapore icon (Esplanade, MBS, MRT train, bus, kopitiam, etc.)
- Kids assemble the puzzle by matching stickers to piece shapes

### Design Direction
- **Totally new** — do NOT follow "Kampung to City" style
- Fresh creative direction — but must feel like a children's educational book
- Target age: young kids (sticker book audience, likely 4-8 years old)
- Print-ready output: correct dimensions, bleed, CMYK-ready
